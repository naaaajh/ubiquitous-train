As disputes boil at G7,
Trump softens on trade war

President Donald Trump says all is well at the Group of 7 summit, which gets underway Sunday on France's Atlantic coast.

But there is evidence of rancor behind the scenes. American officials are griping the yearly gathering is being orchestrated to isolate Trump. Trade disputes are spilling into the open, even with a new friend. And Trump is disputing agreements between the leaders after they are announced.

Already, the near-consensus among Trump's foreign counterparts that tariffs on China are causing the global economy to slump appeared to weigh on the President. He told reporters he's had his own misgivings.
